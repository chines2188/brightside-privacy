☀ Bright Side — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Bright Side ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Bright Side is built with privacy first. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or personal identifiers
  • Your location
  • Your browsing or reading history
  • Any data for advertising or tracking purposes

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your Gratitude Journal entries
  • Saved stories
  • Your profile name and preferences

3. User-Generated Content (Sparks)

If you choose to share a Spark (a positive moment), that content is submitted to our service so it can appear in the app for other users. Please do not include personal information in Sparks you share publicly.

4. AI-Generated Content

Stories in Bright Side are generated using Claude, an AI by Anthropic. Story generation requests are processed by Anthropic's API. No personal user data is included in these requests.

5. In-App Purchases

Purchases (Bright Side Pro subscription and tips) are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

6. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites.

7. Children's Privacy

Bright Side is rated 9+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
